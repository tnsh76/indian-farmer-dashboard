Data cleaning notes

• A summary "Total" row was mixed into the per-video table — excluded from all video-level breakdowns and used only for channel-wide KPIs.
• The per-video list (498 rows) sums to ~43.6M views, about 2.8% below the reported channel total of 44.88M — this export appears to exclude a long tail of very-low-view videos, so video-level splits (Shorts vs. long-form, top lists) reflect ~97% of total views, not 100%.
• 2 videos show a negative net subscriber count (more unsubscribes than subscribes while watching) — kept as-is, this is a valid YouTube metric, not an error.
• 1 video has 0 recorded impressions, making its click-through rate mathematically undefined (blank) rather than 0% — left blank rather than defaulted to zero.
• Impressions/CTR are only reported by YouTube for 5 of 19 traffic sources — the rest are structurally blank, not missing data.
• The "Chart data" tab tracks daily trends for only 5 individual videos (not all 498); the "Totals" tab has only a daily average-view-duration series, with no daily Views/Watch-time breakdown — so day-by-day trending below the whole-channel level is limited by the source export, not a cleaning choice.
• The brief mentions a "Courses platform" — no such data exists in this file; course activity was inferred from the 4 videos whose titles reference the course.
